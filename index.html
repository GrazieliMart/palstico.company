Recreating with Recycled Plastic

Through Plastico, you can access recycled materials to build your entire project looking forward to a greener future.

Plastico is committed to sustainability and transforming waste into opportunity. We specialize in turning discarded plastic into high-quality sheets and panels that can be used for furniture, construction materials, and other innovative projects. Our goal is to inspire change and contribute to a circular economy.
